About Me

Hi! I’m Geneva, a recent grad from Rutgers University, currently based in New Jersey.

I’ve been interested in design for as long as I can remember. I first expressed this through fashion when my mom first let me pick out my own outfits. A little later in life I found more creative outlets like nail art and crochet.

I found a passion for coding in college when I realized I could mix art and code to create a beautiful experience for others.
